The Paradigm Shift

Why traditional healthcare education needs an upgrade.

⚠️

The Problem

  • ✖ Static Text: Traditional case studies fail to capture dynamic physiological realities.
  • ✖ Missing Context: They don't accurately reflect how Social Determinants of Health (SDOH) biologically impact patients over time.
  • ✖ High Friction: Instructors spend excessive hours drafting and tailoring cases for multiple professions.
💡

The Opportunity

Leveraging PFun's physiofunctional simulation API to generate realistic patient data tailored to ethical or environmental "parameters".

Creates a living simulation forcing students to ask:
"Whose problem is this: The Individual, The Organization, or Society?"

Product Architecture

Three core modules working in synergy to provide a comprehensive educational experience.

The Case Generator

Instructors use a simple interface to design or modify patient scenarios rapidly.

🏵️ Modifiable Parameters

Toggle socio-economic status, insurance, medication adherence, and behaviors (e.g., smoking).

📈 Dynamic Data

PFun API generates a realistic 14-day physiological dataset (CGM, Cortisol) based on inputs.

🎯 Objectives Mapping

Align cases with IP competencies: Roles/Responsibilities, Values/Ethics, Communication, Teamwork.

Interprofessional Portal

Students log into the same case but view it through their specific professional lens to collaborate on a Plan of Care.

Critical Cues Dashboard

Highlight discipline-specific cues (e.g., PT notes med timing; Social Worker notes insurance lapse).

Cross-Disciplinary Collaboration

Compare notes, identify overlapping responsibilities, formulate unified plans.

The "Realms" Framework

Analyze cases across 1) Individual (Patient-Clinician), 2) Organizational (Hospital/Insurance), 3) Societal (Public Health).

The Community Bulletin

A forum-like marketplace for educators to share, discuss, and refine.

📢

Share modified cases, discuss learning outcomes, and build a repository of "living" case studies across universities.
